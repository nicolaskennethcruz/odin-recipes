Chicken Pesto

Description

This creamy chicken pesto pasta is rich, flavorful, and incredibly easy to make. Tender, juicy chicken pairs perfectly with pasta coated in a smooth, herb-packed pesto sauce.

The savory depth of the chicken contrasts beautifully with the fresh, garlicky brightness of the pesto, creating a bold mix of flavors that somehow come together in delicious harmony with every bite.

Comforting, vibrant, and satisfying all at once — it’s the kind of dish that keeps you going back for more.

Ingredients

  • 1kg Chicken Breast
  • 1kg Penne Pasta
  • 500g Pesto Paste
  • 350ml Evaporatred Milk
  • Parsley
  • 4tbsp Oil
  • 2tbsp Salt
  • 2tbsp Pepper
  • 2tbsp Garlic Powder
  • 2tbsp Paprika
  • 1tbsp Sugar

Steps

Chicken

  1. Dice or slice the chicken breast into bite-sized pieces
  2. Place the chicken in a bowl and season with salt, pepper, garlic powder, paprika, a little oil, and pesto paste. Mix thoroughly to be well coated
  3. Cover and refrigirate for at least 2 hours to marinate
  4. Heat a pan to high heat and add a small amount of oil. Once hot, add the chicken
  5. Wait until it's golden brown then flip
  6. Set aside

Sauce

  1. Add the remaining pesto paste to the same pan used to cook the chicken/li>
  2. Saute in high heat for about 2 minutes
  3. Pour in the evaporated milk gradually while stirring
  4. Mix well until the pesto and milk fully combine and the texture becomes smooth and sauce-like
  5. Taste the sauce and adjust seasoning as needed. Add a small amount of salt or fish sauce to deepen the flavor, then a little brown sugar to balance the richness and sharpness

Penne Pasta

  1. Boil the pasta according to the cooking time on the package
  2. About 2 minutes before it reaches doneness drain and transfer the pasta directly into the sauce to finish cooking
  3. Top with the cooked chicken and mix lightly or leave some pieces on top
  4. Sprinkle with chopped parsley before serving
