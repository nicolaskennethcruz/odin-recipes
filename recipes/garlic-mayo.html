Garlic Mayo

Description

This garlic mayo dip is creamy and savory. This is blended with fresh garlic, a touch of citrus or vinegar, and simple seasonings.

This is rich in flavor and a bit garlicky - perfect for dipping fries, meats, or seafoods.

Ingredients

  • 500g Real Mayonnaise
  • A bit of Garlic Powder
  • 250ml All Purpose Cream
  • 5tbsp Lemon Juice
  • Minced fresh Garlic
  • A bit of Pepper

Steps

  1. In a bowl, combine the mayonnaise and all-purpose cream and mix well until smooth
  2. Add the lemon juice and stir until fully blended
  3. Add the minced fresh garlic and a small amount of garlic powder
  4. Season with a bit of pepper, then mix everything well
  5. Taste and adjust garlic or lemon juice if needed
