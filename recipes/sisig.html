Sisig

Description

This sisig is seasoned with calamansi, onions, and chili. This is savory, tangy, and a bit spicy, with a mix

of crispy and tender textures. It is also perfect with rice or as a pulutan

Ingredients

  • 1kg Pork
  • 500g Real Mayonnaise
  • 3tbsp Oyster Sauce
  • 2tbsp Liquid Seasoning
  • 78g Liver Spread
  • 4tbsp Calamansi Juice
  • 1 whole White Onion
  • 6 cloves Garlic
  • 200g Margarine
  • A bit of Pepper
  • A bit of Salt
  • 3pcs Siling Green

Steps

Pork

  1. Boil the meat in water with pepper, garlic, onion, and salt until it becomes tender
  2. Set it aside and let it cool completely
  3. Fry over medium heat until golden brown
  4. Let it cool down for at least 5 minutes, then fry again over high heat for 1 minute

Sauce

  1. In a bowl, mix the oyster sauce, mayonnaise, calamansi juice, liquid seasoning, liver spread, and pepper. Set aside
  2. In a pan with oil and margarine, saut√© the garlic, onion, and green chili
  3. Add the sauce mixture and let it simmer for at least 3 minutes
  4. Add the meat and mix well
